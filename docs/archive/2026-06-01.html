supply chain npm authentication

This package has been downloaded over 29,000 times and is still available for download, raising concerns about its impact on developers.
  • The compromised authentication tokens can allow attackers to gain unauthorized access to developers' projects and sensitive data, potentially leading to further exploitation. As the package remains available, the risk of additional downloads and subsequent breaches increases.

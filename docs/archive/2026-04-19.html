NAKIVO v11.2: Ransomware Defense, Faster Replication, vSphere 9, and Proxmox VE 9.0 Support

The release of NAKIVO Backup & Replication v11.2 introduces critical ransomware defense features that organizations must adopt to safeguard their data now.

Who should care: IT teams managing data backup and recovery processes, especially those using VMware and Proxmox environments.
Attack path: Attackers may exploit vulnerabilities in backup solutions to encrypt or delete backups, hindering recovery efforts. Enhanced ransomware defenses in this update aim to prevent such exploitation.
Why this matters now: Useful context for teams tracking where attacker pressure is building.
In plain English: NAKIVO has launched a new version of its backup software with enhanced ransomware protection and support for new virtualization platforms.
ransomware backup virtualization
  • NAKIVO Inc. has announced the general availability of Backup & Replication v11.2, which includes new features aimed at improving data protection against ransomware. The update also adds support for vSphere 9 and Proxmox VE 9.0, enhancing compatibility with modern virtualization environments.
  • Ransomware attacks are increasingly sophisticated, making robust backup solutions essential for data recovery. The new features in v11.2 can help organizations mitigate the risk of data loss and ensure business continuity in the event of an attack.

Analysis of 216M Security Findings Shows a 4x Increase In Critical Risk (2026 Report)

The alarming 400% increase in critical security risks underscores an urgent need for organizations to reassess their vulnerability management strategies.

Who should care: Security teams across all organizations, particularly those utilizing AI technologies, should be vigilant.
Attack path: Attackers may exploit these high-impact vulnerabilities more easily due to the increasing volume of alerts, potentially leading to successful breaches.
Why this matters now: Useful context for teams tracking where attacker pressure is building.
In plain English: A recent analysis reveals a significant rise in critical security risks, driven by rapid AI development.
vulnerabilities ai security
  • OX Security analyzed 216 million security findings from 250 organizations over a 90-day period. The report indicates a 52% year-over-year increase in raw alert volume, with critical risks surging by nearly 400%.
  • This trend suggests that organizations are facing a growing number of high-impact vulnerabilities that could lead to severe breaches if not addressed promptly. The rapid pace of AI-assisted development is contributing to a 'velocity gap,' making it harder for security teams to keep up.
  • Attackers may exploit these high-impact vulnerabilities more easily due to the increasing volume of alerts, potentially leading to successful breaches.
